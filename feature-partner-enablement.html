Partner Enablement

Scale partner programs without scaling partner operations. Onboard new partners faster, settle accurately, and maintain control across coalition, earn, and burn relationships. One platform for the entire partner lifecycle.

Schedule Demo
[Partner Ecosystem Diagram]

100 Partners. 100 Different Agreements.

Partnerships are where loyalty programs get complicated. Every partner brings a different agreement, a different operating cadence, and a different data format. Point-to-point integrations create technical debt. Manual reconciliation creates billing disputes. The cost compounds with every partner you add.

Native Support for Every Partnership Model

Coalition Partners

Multiple brands. One currency. Members earn and redeem across the coalition while each partner maintains separate rules and funding.

Earn Partners

Members earn your currency through partner activity. Attribution tracks to source for accurate settlement.

Burn Partners

Members redeem at partner locations. Reimbursement calculated automatically per agreement terms.

Co-Funded Promotions

Partners co-fund promotions. Allocation and settlement tracked per party. Members see one offer.

Partner Governance

Each Partner Is a Micro-Program

Every partner operates as its own micro-program within RCX. Segregated rules, access control, and agreements per partner. Partner activity is evaluated, governed, and measured using the same enterprise execution model as your own first-party transactions.

  • Separate rules per partner without separate systems
  • Partner activity isolated for compliance and billing accuracy
  • Reimbursement calculated per agreement, even when rates change mid-period

Batch Gateway

Onboard Partners in Hours, Not Weeks

Every partner sends data differently. FeedXChange handles format translation, validation, and response generation through reusable templates. Your team configures once. New partners onboard in hours.

  • Reusable templates for faster partner onboarding
  • Automated monitoring and alerting on every job
  • Full history with downloadable files for audit

Batch and Real-Time. Same Rules. Same Outcomes.

Partner files process through the same rules engine as real-time transactions. Consistent behavior. Consistent logging. Accurate billing regardless of how data arrives.

Sub-Balance Tracking

Track Partner Value Separately. Settle Accurately.

Members see one balance. Finance sees attribution by partner. When members redeem, the platform debits the correct funding source automatically.

  • Members see a unified balance; finance sees partner attribution
  • Redemptions debit the correct funding source per business rules
  • Full audit trail from earn to redeem
