Open Architecture

Your Data. Your Tools. Your Way.

If you want to go deeper, you have unfettered SQL access to the raw data. Most of our larger clients sync the full data model into their own data lake. The data model is extensive, about 100 tables. We want to feed the data over to your other systems. We take the opposite approach of vendors who silo data.

  • Full SQL access to the underlying data warehouse
  • Sync to client data lakes (Snowflake, Redshift, BigQuery)
  • Integration with existing BI tools (Tableau, Looker, Power BI)
  • Streaming events via Kafka, batch exports, or warehouse queries
