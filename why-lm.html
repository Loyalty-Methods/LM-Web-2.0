Personalization

Beyond Segments. Down to the Individual.

Segments group people who aren't the same. We personalize promotion parameters, not just eligibility. That's how a program becomes one to one.

  • Tailor spend targets to individual appetite so every member sees a goal they can hit
  • Filter offers by real-time location so members only see what's relevant right now
  • Show the full rewards catalog including locked items with unlock messaging that drives progression
  • Personalize promotion parameters per member so two people in the same tier get different experiences
