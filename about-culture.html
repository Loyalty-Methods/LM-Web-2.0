Loyalty Is What We Build Together

At Loyalty Methods, people are at the center of everything we do. Whether you evolve ReactorCX in a technical role or help bring loyalty programs to life for our clients, you see your work live in the hands of millions of members every day.

Our Core Values

Principles that guide us

These principles guide how we evolve ReactorCX, run loyalty programs for our clients, and show up for each other every day.

Collaboration

We solve hard problems together. No one is left alone with a critical issue. Teams share context early and review each other's ideas.

Innovation

We are builders at heart. We look for simpler, smarter ways to handle complex logic and fold what we learn back into ReactorCX.

Integrity

Our work touches real revenue. That demands honesty. We say what is true, document what we have done, and own the outcome.

Growth

We expect people to grow along with the platform. Feedback is frequent, and there is room to learn new technologies and own more.

Inclusivity

Every voice is welcomed. We work across time zones and cultures, ensuring decisions don't depend on being in the loudest room.

Passion

We care about the craft and the result. Passion shows up in thoughtful design, clean data models, and celebrating wins together.
