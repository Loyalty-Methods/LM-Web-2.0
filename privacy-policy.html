Loyalty Methods Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 2024
Last Updated: December 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Loyalty Methods (“we”, “our”, “us”). Protecting your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we gather, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website, https://www.loyaltymethods.com

Information We Collect

Usage Data:

When you interact with our website, we may collect certain information about your visit to help improve your experience. This may include:

  • Device and browser type
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited on our site
  • Time and date of access
  • Referring URLs

How We Use the Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide support.
  • Enhance, personalize, and improve the functionality of our website and services.
  • Analyze website performance and user behavior to identify areas for improvement.
  • Maintain the security and integrity of our website.
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraudulent activity.

How We Share Information

We do not sell or trade information to external parties. However, we may share information with trusted third-party service providers for purposes such as website maintenance, analytics, or technical support. These providers are bound by agreements to ensure data is used solely for these purposes.

In certain circumstances, information may be disclosed to comply with legal requirements or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or others.

Data Security

We employ a variety of administrative, technical, and physical measures to secure the data collected through our website. While we strive to protect all information, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

Third-Party Links

Our website may include links to third-party websites for your convenience. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before engaging with their services.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we handle information.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@loyaltymethods.com.
