Advanced Promotion Mechanics

Launch complex promotions in days, not quarters. BOGO, streaks, challenges, mix & match, partner co-funding. Configured by your team, live without custom code.

Multi-Brand Programs

Operate multiple loyalty programs without multiple platforms. Separate brands, separate tier structures, separate rules. Unified management.

Gamification That Drives Frequency

Increase visit frequency with streaks that reward repeat behavior. Members commit to actions over time and return to complete them. Targets are set based on previous engagement.

Conversational Analytics
