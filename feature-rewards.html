Customers expect rewards in the moment. RCX processes earn and burn transactions in real-time, so members can redeem points immediately—at checkout, at the pump, or in the app.

  • Instant reward fulfillment with zero lag
  • Real-time notifications on points earned and available rewards
  • Fraud detection and reversal capabilities
