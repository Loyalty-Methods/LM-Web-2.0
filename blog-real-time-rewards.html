Rewards & Benefits

Deliver flexible redemption options that keep members engaged. From points and perks to exclusive experiences, reward customers how they want, when they want.

REWARDS & BENEFITS [16:9]
In This Article
Flexible Redemption Options Real-Time Redemption Omnichannel Wallet Dynamic Catalog Tier Management Every Reward Type

Flexible Redemption Options

Give customers the freedom to redeem their loyalty currency across every channel and touchpoint.

Points & Currency

Multi-currency wallets with real-time conversion, vesting schedules, and automated expiration management. Members see exactly what they have and what they can do with it.

Tiered Benefits

Exclusive perks, priority access, and status rewards that scale with member engagement. Higher tiers unlock differentiated value that drives continued participation.

Split & Linked Pay

Let members combine points with cash, credit cards, or gift cards for frictionless checkout experiences. Removing payment friction increases redemption rates.

Experiences & Events

Offer VIP access, early releases, exclusive merchandise, and money-can't-buy moments. Experiential rewards create emotional connection without eroding margin.

Real-Time Redemption

Instant Gratification

Customers expect rewards in the moment. RCX processes earn and burn transactions in real time, so members can redeem points immediately: at checkout, at the pump, or in the app.

Batch processing creates lag. Lag creates friction. Friction kills engagement. Real-time processing eliminates that chain.

Capabilities:

  • Variable burn rates for dynamic pricing strategies
  • Instant reward fulfillment with zero lag
  • Real-time notifications on points earned and available rewards
  • Fraud detection and reversal capabilities

Omnichannel Wallet

Earn Anywhere. Redeem Everywhere.

Your members engage across mobile, web, in-store, and partner locations. RCX ensures a consistent earn and burn experience across every customer touchpoint.

When wallets are fragmented by channel, members lose track of balances and benefits. Unified wallets consolidate everything into a single view, regardless of where the transaction occurred.

Capabilities:

  • Unified member wallet across all channels
  • Mobile app, kiosk, and web property support
  • Partner location earn and redemption
  • Offline transaction sync for zero downtime

Dynamic Catalog

Build Your Reward Marketplace

From merchandise and discounts to charitable donations and experiential rewards, RCX gives you the flexibility to design a reward catalog that resonates with your audience.

Static catalogs go stale. Dynamic catalogs let you rotate offerings by season, segment, or tier without engineering involvement.

Capabilities:

  • Curated reward collections by segment or tier
  • Third-party reward catalog integrations
  • Limited-time and seasonal reward offerings
  • Self-service reward management portal

Tier Management

Status That Drives Behavior

Multi-tier programs with tier-specific benefits, exclusive rewards, accelerated earning rates, and priority access. Design differentiated value propositions for each tier to drive member progression.

Tiers work when the gap between levels is meaningful but achievable. RCX lets you define qualification rules, manage requalification windows, and handle downgrades gracefully.

Capabilities:

  • Flexible tier qualification and requalification rules
  • Tier-exclusive benefits and rewards
  • Accelerated earning rates by tier
  • Grace periods and soft landings for tier downgrades

Every Reward Type, One Platform

Discounts & Coupons

Percentage off, dollars off, BOGO, and category-specific discounts applied at checkout. Rules engine handles stacking logic and exclusions.

Free Products

Reward members with complimentary items from your product catalog or partner offerings. Inventory integration ensures availability before issuance.

Gift Cards

Digital and physical gift cards from your brand or an extensive network of third-party retailers. Members choose what matters to them.

Experiences

VIP access, backstage passes, early releases, and money-can't-buy moments for top members. Experiential rewards differentiate your program.

Charitable Donations

Let members donate points to causes they care about. Donation options strengthen emotional brand connection without direct cost to margin.

Sweepstakes & Contests

Entry-based rewards that create excitement and drive engagement with high-value prizes. Sweepstakes extend perceived value beyond your reward budget.

A loyalty rewards platform is enterprise software that manages point accrual, redemption, tier progression, and benefit delivery for customer loyalty programs. It connects to your existing systems (POS, CRM, e-commerce) and processes member transactions in real time. The platform tracks liability, handles fulfillment, and provides the rules engine that governs how members earn and burn.

Real-time redemption means point balances update instantly when a transaction occurs. When a member makes a purchase, the platform calculates earned points, evaluates tier status, checks promotion eligibility, and updates the wallet before the receipt prints. This eliminates the overnight batch processing that creates balance discrepancies and member confusion.
