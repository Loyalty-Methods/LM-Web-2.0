Program Design & Rules

Define how members earn, spend, and progress through your program. Configure earn rules, burn rules, tier logic, and promotion mechanics through a structured rules engine.

Schedule Demo
[Program Rules Builder Interface]

Every Rule Starts with Four Questions

Who qualifies. Where it applies. When it's active. What triggers it.

Who

Target by member attributes, tier, segment, preferences, or status.

Where

Filter by location, brand, category, or outlet list.

When

Control by effective dates, day of week, hour of day, or time zone.

What

Define by product, service, activity type, or transaction attribute.

Earning Logic

Define How Value Enters the Program

Configure base earn rates, bonus multipliers, and conditional logic for any activity type. Every earn event creates a traceable accrual record with full lineage.

  • Base rates by spend, visit, stay, or custom metric
  • Multipliers by tier, segment, location, or time window
  • Partner earn rules with separate attribution
  • Real-time balance updates across all channels
[Redemption Flow Example]
Redemption Logic

Control How Value Gets Used

Define redemption rules, burn order, and consumption logic. Track every redemption back to the original accrual for finance-grade traceability.

  • Burn rules by reward type, location, or channel
  • FIFO or custom burn order by tag, category, or recency
  • Escrow and deferred redemption patterns
  • Full lineage from redemption to original earn
