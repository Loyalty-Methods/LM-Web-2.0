Every Rule Starts with Four Questions

Who qualifies. Where it applies. When it's active. What triggers it.

Who

Target by member attributes, tier, segment, preferences, or status.

Where

Filter by location, brand, category, or outlet list.

When

Control by effective dates, day of week, hour of day, or time zone.

What

Define by product, service, activity type, or transaction attribute.
