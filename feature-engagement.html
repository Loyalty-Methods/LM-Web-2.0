Activities, Not Just Transactions

We use "activities" instead of "transactions" because it can be any type of engagement you want to reward. Visits. App logins. Survey answers. Profile completions. Review submissions. The platform treats every signal the same way: ingest it, evaluate it against your rules, and respond in real time. These non-transactional features are valuable for a reason. Members are telling you directly what they want, who they are, what they're interested in. We're essentially using points to buy that information.

Non-Transactional Engagement

Reward Any Member Behavior

Every interaction is an opportunity to learn and engage.

Surveys & Polls

Reward members for sharing opinions, preferences, and feedback. Structure questions to capture exactly what you need for segmentation and personalization.

Profile Completion

Start with minimal enrollment friction. Earn more as you tell us more. Each attribute collected flows directly into the rules engine for immediate use in targeting and offers.

App Activity

App opens, feature usage, content views, check-ins. Track and reward the behaviors that signal interest and intent.

Reviews & Ratings

Reward members for leaving reviews on purchases, stays, or experiences. Feed sentiment data into your analytics and segmentation.

Social Sharing

Reward members for sharing content, promotions, or achievements. Track attribution and measure social engagement ROI.

Check-Ins

Digital or physical check-ins at locations, events, or properties. Combine with geo-location APIs for context-aware rewards.

The Most Valuable Data Comes Directly From Members

Zero-party data is information members share intentionally: preferences, interests, feedback, intentions. Unlike inferred data or third-party sources, it comes with explicit consent and clear meaning. The platform captures it through engagement mechanics and feeds it immediately into your rules engine. Better segments. Better personalization. Better offers. All from information members volunteered.

Acquisition Engine

Program-Powered Word of Mouth

Referrals are a built-in acquisition engine with measurable ROI. When members hear about your program from someone they trust, they're more likely to act. The platform supports three referral approaches, each optimized for different objectives.

Approach How It Works Best For
Viral Shareable codes or links. Broad reach, low friction. Scale and awareness
Personalized Unique member-specific links with full attribution Quality and tracking
Progressive Deferred rewards until qualification (join â†’ transact â†’ qualify) High-value acquisition

Progressive referrals are particularly effective. You're not paying for names in a database. You're paying for outcomes: a friend joins, they transact, conditions are met, then rewards unlock. Two-sided rewards with deferred qualification mean higher-quality acquisition.

Engagement Mechanics

Mechanics That Build Repeat Habits

Gamification is designed to deepen engagement and build the repeat habits that increase frequency and purchase volume. Getting members to do something over a period of time and get rewarded for it. The platform supports the full range of mechanics.

  • Badges for visual recognition of achievements
  • Time-based challenges with progress tracking
  • Earn previews showing members what they'll earn before checkout
  • Sweepstakes entries earned through activity
  • Status match challenges for tier qualification
  • Unlock chains and level progression

Personalize the Targets, Not Just the Offer

A hospitality brand uses hyper-personalized streaks where both the spend targets AND the rewards are personalized based on what members have actually done. Through data analysis, the platform gauges who has appetite to spend what amounts. Two members might see the same streak with completely different thresholds. Some people see a generic target and think "I'm never going to spend that much." Personalized targets meet members where they are.

Event-Driven Architecture

Members Know the Moment Something Happens

The platform is heavily event-driven. Anything that happens generates real-time events that you can use to notify members immediately. You achieved a streak. You got a bonus. You hit a new tier. Those events come out on our event backbone and get pushed immediately. Congratulations screens. Instant notifications. Members get that satisfaction right away.

  • 27+ built-in event types
  • Real-time streaming via Kafka, webhooks, Azure EventHub
  • App overlays triggered the moment conditions are met
  • Push notifications, SMS, email triggered by events
  • Sub-second event publishing

Data Enrichment

Start Simple. Learn Over Time.
