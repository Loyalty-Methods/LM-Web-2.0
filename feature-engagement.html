Zero-Party Data

The Most Valuable Data Comes Directly From Members

Zero-party data is information members share intentionally: preferences, interests, feedback, intentions. Unlike inferred data or third-party sources, it comes with explicit consent and clear meaning. The platform captures it through engagement mechanics and feeds it immediately into your rules engine.

Beyond Transactions

Activities, Not Just Transactions

We use "activities" instead of "transactions" because it can be any type of engagement you want to reward. Visits. App logins. Survey answers. Profile completions. Review submissions. The platform treats every signal the same way: ingest it, evaluate it against your rules, and respond in real time.

Acquisition Engine

Program-Powered Word of Mouth

Referrals are a built-in acquisition engine with measurable ROI. When members hear about your program from someone they trust, they are more likely to act. The platform supports three referral approaches, each optimized for different objectives.

Approach How It Works
Instant Referrer earns immediately when referee enrolls.
Progressive Rewards unlock as the referee hits activity milestones.
Dual-Sided Both referrer and referee earn at each stage.
