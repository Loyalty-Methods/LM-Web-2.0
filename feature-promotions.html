How It Works

Every Transaction. Evaluated Instantly.

RCX evaluates every discount in real time at checkout. When multiple promotions qualify, the engine selects the optimal combination based on your policies. Sub-second response times at enterprise volume. No batch processing. No cached logic.

  • Real-time evaluation at point of sale for every transaction
  • Sub-second response times at enterprise scale
  • Instant balance updates across all channels
  • Every decision logged for audit and dispute resolution
  • Configurable optimization — best for member, best for margin, or balanced
