Front-End Personalization

Every Member Sees Their Own Reality

The platform supports real-time queries that return a complete, personalized view for each member. What's my status? What promotions am I eligible for? What rewards can I get? What can't I get, and why not? How far am I from the next tier? What are my calls to action?

One query. Full context. Filtered for that member. The response is not a generic dashboard. It's their view of the world inside your program.

  • Full catalog with eligibility status per member
  • "Why not" messaging explains qualification gaps
  • Unlock criteria creates calls to action
  • Status, progress, and projected outcomes in one response

Back-End Personalization

Members Know the Moment Something Happens

The platform is heavily event-driven. Anything that happens generates real-time events that you can use to engage members immediately. You achieved a streak. You got a bonus. You hit a new tier.

Those events come out on the event backbone and get pushed the moment conditions are met. Congratulations screens. Instant notifications. App overlays. Members get that satisfaction right away.

  • 27+ built-in event types
  • Sub-second event publishing
  • Push notifications, SMS, email triggered by events
  • App overlays triggered the moment conditions are met

Individual-Level Personalization

Personalize the Targets, Not Just the Offer

This is the third dimension, and it's where things get interesting. The platform can personalize promotion parameters down to the individual member.

A hospitality client uses hyper-personalized streaks where both the spend targets AND the rewards are tailored based on what members have actually done. Through data analysis, the platform gauges who has appetite to spend what amounts. Some people see a generic target and think "I'm never going to spend that much." Personalized targets meet members where they are.

  • Spend targets personalized per member
  • Reward values scaled to individual behavior
  • Same mechanic, different thresholds
  • Targets based on historical patterns and appetite
[Side-by-side comparison showing same streak with different personalized targets]

Show What's Locked. Explain Why. Watch Behavior Change.

Most platforms hide content members aren't eligible for. ReactorCX takes the opposite approach. A retail client saw significant revenue lift from one change: showing age-restricted offers as locked with a verification prompt instead of hiding them entirely. Members who could qualify but didn't know they needed to verify suddenly completed the process and converted. Calls to action instead of erasure. That's the unlock principle applied across the entire member experience.
